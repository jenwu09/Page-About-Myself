My Hobbies

Journaling

I enjoy journaling as a way to destress. My journal is often decorated with washi tapes, stickers, and images that relate to the content. I typically write entries when something fun or interesting happens, like hanging out with friends. During stressful times, I turn to journaling to write it all out, using it as a form of meditation and reflection.

Dancing

I find going to the gym a bit intimidating, so instead, I dance at home as an alternative workout. I usually follow a playlist of K-pop dances for cardio. The YouTube playlist includes dance practices, and I simply follow along. It's a fun and energetic way to stay active.
